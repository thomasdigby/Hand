What is hand?

hand is an online art and design gallery catering for students, individuals and groups across all art disciplines, creating space for inspiration, collaboration and discovery.

Exhibiting

In some cases, exhibitions are viewed by only a small percentage of the population. As a first of its kind in online innovation, hand will help exhibitions reach a wider audience, creating a new dialogue between artist and industry.

Viewing

Every exhibition on hand can be viewed free of charge at your convenience, making art and design accessible to those seeking new talent, inspiration and opportunities for collaboration. The site will include regular features, news and reviews as well as interviews with both exhibitors and industry experts. In addition to student exhibitions, non-student shows will be featured on the site throughout the year.

Our vision

hand will pioneer the future of art and design galleries, reimagining the concept of exhibitions. It will create a show space exclusively for online exhibiting or mirroring of a physical show. We also plan to create an immersive, interactive space that combines the physical with the digital; it is this crossover between the virtual and the physical hand seeks to challenge.

Above all, hand aims to share art with as many people as possible, inspire others and connect a community of artists within a place for collaboration, events and discovery.

How to be involved

hand is a user-based platform allowing exhibitors to create an account and upload exhibitions, significantly increasing the potential audience for all exhibitions shared via the site. Viewers can also create an account to bookmark exhibitions, work and artists of interest completely free of charge however it is not essential to have an account in order to view shows.

Our standards

Before each exhibition goes live, hand will ensure the show meets the high standards expected of any art gallery. For further information on exhibiting on hand, please contact info@hand.gallery
