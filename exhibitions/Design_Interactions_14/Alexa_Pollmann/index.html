    Alexa Pollmann
    Indivicracy (2014)

    www.alexapollmann.de
    i@alexapollmann.de

    Indivicracy is the tale of a new form of government which presents diverse characters gathered under a non-territorial statehood. Based on today’s increasing occurrence of nomadic life-styles, the plot of the tale revolves around the cultural rise of migratory existence: body-vehicles become national costumes and flexibility the new ideal.
