    Marcel Helmer
    Prospects of a Chair (2014)

    www.marcelhelmer.de
    helloworld@marcelhelmer.de

    A graphic representation of a spacial installation proposal. Several rows of a daily object funded on the same rules and similarities, yet different and unique. Some functional, some fictional, some fantastic. Prospects of a chair is meant to visualize the possibilities and strength of digital prototyping and manufacturing.
