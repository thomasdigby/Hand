    Eve Somerville
    Isn’t My House Classic? (2014)

    www.evesomerville.com
    evesomerville@gmail.com

    Presented in chronological order ‘Isn’t my house classic?’ features screenshots from 1978 to present day, commenting on the predictability of Hollywood teen movies. The book is named after Clueless’ heroine Cher Horowitz commenting, “isn’t my house classic? The columns date all the way back to 1972.
