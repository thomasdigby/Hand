    Oliver Merchant
    Unnatural Sight (2014)

    www.cargocollective.com/olivermerchant
    ollie.merchant@gmail.com

    These photographs were made with the use of a microscope. This project explores the hidden beauty outside our natural sight, secret views that are only revealed by an optical instrument. Presented as a series of plates, this book reflects the microscope slides that the photographs were made from.
