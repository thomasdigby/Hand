    James Callison
    The Hard Season (2014)

    www.jamescallisonphoto.com
    jamesfcallison@gmail.com

    The Hard Season is a study of allotments over a period of three months. I feel today we forget how privileged we are, when you can walk into a supermarket and pick up fresh produce sourced from all over the world all year round. I set out to investigate what happens on this plots of lands once the summer seasons have past.
