    Sarah Watkins
    Back In Salone (2014)

    www.sarahlouisewatkins.weebly.com
    sarahlouise.watkins@yahoo.com

    These images are a culmination of an eight-day visit to Sierra Leone, West Africa, in loving memory of my father Paul Watkins. My personal connection with this beautiful country is through my parents work for Voluntary Service Overseas in the 1980′s. I have always been keen to visit the country and retrace their steps and since the recent passing of my father I have been even more determined to visit. I felt this would be a brilliant opportunity to document the place they first met and spent their first few years together.
