    Hannah Barney
    Vatn (2014)

    www.hannahibarney.com
    hannahingabarney@gmail.com

    ‘Vatn’ is a photographic exploration of capturing the physicality of the Icelandic landscape. Submerged in water, the camera acts as a vessel for this transaction and allows us to look further into what the unreachable landscape has to offer.
