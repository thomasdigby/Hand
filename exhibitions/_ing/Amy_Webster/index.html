    STITCH: An Infographic (2014)

    www.arwdesign.com
    amyroseww@hotmail.com

    STITCH is a curatorial brand founded by Lily Bonesso, Josie Tucker and myself. In January 2014 we curated an exhibition of 6 artists’ work at the Cock ‘n’ Bull Gallery, London. This double sided infographic poster maps the detail of the entire project - revealing behind the scenes - over 669 days.
