    Desk tops as Desktops (2014)

    www.cargocollective.com/kirstieballard
    kirstieballard@hotmail.com

    Desk tops as Desktops is a proposal for a computer platform in which users can upload an aerial photograph of their physical desk top or workspace and use it as the desktop on their computer. It allows users to link objects based on their own personal associations and experiences.
