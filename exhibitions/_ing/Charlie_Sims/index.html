    Charlie Sims
    Sin City (2014)

    www.charliesims.co.uk
    hello@charliesims.co.uk

    Sin City is a contemporary appropriation of the traditional seven deadly sins. Using the original sins, I matched them up to a modern national statistic and researched these seven factors for every county in England. Using this data I have then created a series of seven maps of England, all completely distorted depending on how sinful each county has been. The bigger it is, the more sin it has committed.
