    Francis North
    Lossy (2014)

    www.francisnorth.com
    hello@francisnorth.com

    A typographic study revealing the loss of information through replication. Through applying a replicative photocopying process, I rendered the world’s most ubiquitous typeface unrecognisable. The project visually demonstrates the term, ‘lossy’ as a print and three-dimensional outcome.
