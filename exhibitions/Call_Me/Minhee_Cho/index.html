    Minhee Cho
    Hear (2014)

    www.cargocollective.com/minheecho
    quaintmin@gmail.com

    This picture book was inspired by a sister and younger brother who played together sitting next to me on a train last summer. They played happily, blocking their ears with tissues. I noticed that the younger brother wore a hearing aid. Moved by this relationship, I became curious of their secret adventures and constructed my own.
