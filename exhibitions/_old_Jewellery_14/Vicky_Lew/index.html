    Vicky Lew
    Flight (2014)

    www.vickylew.com
    lew.vicky@gmail.com

    Flight is inspired by the drama and majesty of the birds in flight. Images of the birds are presented using pavé set gems and perspective localized, demonstrating the elusive flight of birds. These pieces do not show the birds at first glance but only show itself at a certain angle. It creates a fleeting scene on the wearer, giving the sparkling gem-set birds movement as the wearer moves.
