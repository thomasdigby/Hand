    Gianna Chan
    Tied Up (2014)

    www.gianna-chan.com
    gianna-chan@outlook.com

    Tied Up Wearable body pieces have been developed due to my inspiration from the Japanese rope bondage which is called ‘Shibari’. It is simply means ‘to tie’. My objective is to bring out the features of ‘Shibari’ through my body pieces. The intention is to capture the moment of sexual activities in Japanese bondage into elegant and wonderful jewellery and accessories.
