    Nicola Chan
    Grypes (2014)

    www.nicolannchan.com
    contact@nicolannchan.com

    For centuries, the sword has acted as man's divine body adornment, the same that jewellery had been for women. My design is inspired by these exquisite antique swords. They define the mysterious relationship between our hands and wielding swords, yet juxtapose the strength of a weapon and beauty of a piece of jewellery.
