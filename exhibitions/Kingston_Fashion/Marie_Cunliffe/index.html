    Marie Cunliffe
    Graduate Collection (2014)

    www.mledesign.co.uk
    marie_cunliffe@hotmail.co.uk

    This collection is inspired by aerial imagery of the earth. It explores another viewpoint. The concept is based on an alternative perspective. This is shown through draped shapes contrasting with structural, heavy weight textiles. The earth's contours, fluidity, textures and colour have been translated into print. Fabrics include coral crushed pleats and cobwebbed wool, with robust metal and plastic garments, which create soft silhouettes in unusual ways.
