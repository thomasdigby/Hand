    Stephanie Smith
    Graduate Collection (2014)

    www.steph-smith.net
    stephsmith092@gmail.com

    Inspired by the notion ‘work or die’, which describes the life of a worker bee. This devotion to ones practice instigated an extensive exploration into the protective wear of a beekeeper, a hobby which requires commitment, perseverance and tact, much like the bee itself. Although the beekeeping suit is simple, there is reason behind every detail, which is critical to the wearer’s safety.
